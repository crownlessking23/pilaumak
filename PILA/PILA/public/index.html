About PILA

Purpose of PILA Web App

PILA (Predictive Intelligent Line Assistant) is designed to streamline queue and inventory management for the UMAK Cooperative. It integrates online and walk-in queueing, AI-assisted support, real-time inventory updates, and notifications to improve efficiency and reduce waiting times for students and staff.

Features of PILA Web App

  • Online queue ticketing
  • Real-time queue tracking
  • AI-assisted support
  • Inventory management and notifications
  • Improved efficiency and faster service

Information about UMAK COOP

The University of Makati Cooperative (UMAK Coop) provides essential goods and services to students. With traditional queueing methods often causing delays, PILA modernizes the process with digital ticketing, live queue tracking, and a smart system for faster, fairer service.

Operating Hours: 8AM to 9PM

Days: Weekdays
